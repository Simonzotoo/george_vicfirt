The Rhythm Behind the Music

Biography

Osei Agyekum George, widely known by his stage name Vicfirth, was born on November 6, 1991, in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. He is the son of legendary gospel artist Maame Tiwaa of Yaw Sarpong and Asomafo fame, and Victor Agyekum Boateng, a renowned drummer of his time.

George began his musical journey at the age of 13 when he joined the gospel band Sanctuary of Grace Incorporated (SOGI). Through mentorship and dedication, he has since gained over 18 years of rich experience as a professional drummer performing, recording, and collaborating with numerous artists and bands across Ghana and beyond.

He is happily married to Rolincia Hesse Agyekum, CEO of Light Clothing Fashion. Vicfirth's versatility spans across multiple genres including gospel, hip hop, R&B, soul, reggae, afrobeat, pop, highlife, and jazz.
